About Epicare

Epicare is an innovative wearable device powered by artificial intelligence to monitor vital signals and environmental factors that can trigger epileptic seizures. It provides real-time alerts and sends emergency notifications to family members, ensuring safety and peace of mind.

How It Works

1. Continuous Monitoring

Pulse, movement, light, and sound levels are tracked using integrated sensors.

2. AI-Based Detection

Our intelligent algorithm analyzes data to detect early signs of risk or seizure activity.

3. Smart Alerts

Users receive immediate vibration warnings, and emergency messages are sent to caregivers with the user’s location.

Key Features

AI Risk Analysis

Real-time machine learning models identify patterns that precede seizures.

Health & Environment Sensors

Monitors body signals and ambient conditions like light and sound intensity.

Emergency Alerts

Instant alerts with GPS location sent to registered family or medical contacts.

Wearable Comfort

Designed for comfort and continuous daily use with minimal interference.
